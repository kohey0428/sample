WHAT IS IELTS?

The international English language testing system (IELTS) is design to help your

study or migrate to a country where English is the native language.

This includes countries such as Australia, Canada, New Zealand, the UK and USA.

Your ability to listen, read, write and speak in English will be assessed during the test.

IELTS is graded on a scale of 1 to 9.

IELTS is jointly owned by the British Couincile IDPA:IELTS Australia and Cambridge assessment English
